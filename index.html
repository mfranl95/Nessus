About

Inspired by the #MeToo Movement, Nessus is a non-profit organization that provides of victims of sexual harassment and sexual assualt in either
the home, school or workplace resources to aid their path to recovery. We aspire to give victims of all ages, race, gender, and sexual orientation not only a safe space, but a place where they can be heard and understood.

-Stacey Young & Josh Lindman
